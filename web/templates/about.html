About SqMGR

SqMGR is a site for managing your sports squares. This site was born out of frustration of having to manage paper football squares and easily keep track of who paid and who didn't.

Project Goals

  • Provide easy to maintain sports squares for people who run squares
  • Provide an easy to use interface for people who want to buy a square
  • Make the site free-to-use
  • Make the source code available and open source.
