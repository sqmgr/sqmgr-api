Free

Unlike other sites that charge upwards of $30 for squares, SqMGR is 100% free. No gimicks. No intrusive ads. Just customize your squares and go!

Customizable

Easily control how you want to operate your squares. You can control the size of the grid depending on the number of expected participants. You can set go-live and deadline dates. You can also restrict the number of squares that can be claimed to support rollover events!

Season/Playoff Persistence

Want to setup squares for an entire season or the playoffs? You can re-use the same claimed squares with new numbers drawn for each week!
